CAUCI COIN WARS

Chinese Association at University of California of Irvine (CAUCI) has a coin collecting fundraiser every winter quarter. The quarter long fundraiser has board officers compete in contest of who can collect the most coins from general members and at the end of the quarter, the bottom three officers are pied by the club! Every penny counts!

RULES

● All bigs/officers carry a "coin jar" on them at all times

● Coins count as positive points

● Dollar Bills count as negative points

● EX: -$5.50 points = $2.50 coins - $8.00 bills

● Bigs CANNOT deny dollar bills

● At the end of fundraiser, bottom 3 bigs will be pied!
